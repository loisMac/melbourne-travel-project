Why Melbourne?

Any day is a good day in Melbourne

Melbourne is a paradise for foodies, known for its exceptional coffee culture and diverse eateries. From trendy laneway cafés to gourmet restaurants, the city offers endless dining experiences, with a focus on fresh, innovative cuisine. Coffee lovers will be in heaven, with Melbourne's specialty brews being some of the best in the world.

Beyond food, Melbourne shines in arts, fashion, and sports. The city’s street art, galleries, and theaters make it a cultural hotspot, while its boutique shopping and high-end fashion districts cater to style enthusiasts. Sports fans will love the energy of events like the Australian Open and Formula 1 Grand Prix, which showcase Melbourne’s passion for sport.

Higher Ground is a stylish Melbourne café known for its modern brunch menu, specialty coffee, and stunning light-filled space in a historic building.

Patricia Coffee Brewers is a hidden gem in Melbourne, offering exceptional coffee in a small, standing-room-only space with a minimalist vibe.

Address

Rear of, 493-495 Little Bourke St
